About

Scientific research suggests that cravings or urges to drink are typically short-lived, often lasting between 3 to 15 minutes. Engaging in an alternative activity during this time can help manage and reduce the urge to drink. This approach is supported by cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques, which emphasize the importance of distraction and engagement in other activities to cope with cravings.

By doing something else, such as the activities provided in this app, individuals can effectively divert their attention away from the urge to drink. This not only helps in the moment but also builds resilience over time, making it easier to handle future cravings.

Incorporating enjoyable and engaging activities can increase the likelihood of success in not drinking, as it provides a positive and immediate alternative to alcohol consumption.
